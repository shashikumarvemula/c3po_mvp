# Welcome to C3PO - Your data’s best friend!

**Effectively Using C3PO: Your Structured Analytics Bot**

C3PO is a powerful analytics bot designed to provide detailed insights into sales data for Breast Cancer drugs. To maximize the value you get from interacting with C3PO, consider the following principles:

**1. Be Specific in Your Queries**
Clearly state the timeframe, product names, or geographic regions you're interested in.
Example: "What were the sales figures for Drug X in Area A during Q2 2023?" 


**2. Use Structured Language**
Phrase your questions in a way that mirrors how data is typically organized.
Example: "Compare the market share of Drug Y across different Regions in Area B." 


**3. Build on Previous Queries**
Reference previous questions to dive deeper into specific areas of interest.
Example: "Based on the previous sales trend, what's the projected growth for Drug Z in the next quarter?"


**4. Explore Different Angles**
Ask for data from various perspectives: by product, region, time period, or customer segment.
Example: "Show me the top-performing drugs in each region for the past year."


**5. Request Data Visualizations**
Ask C3PO to present data in charts or graphs when appropriate.
Example: "Can you provide a bar chart comparing the sales of the top 5 drugs over the last 3 quarters?"


**6. Seek Explanations**
Don't hesitate to ask for clarification on trends or anomalies in the data.
Example: "What factors contributed to the spike in sales for Drug A in July?"


**7. Combine Qualitative and Quantitative Queries**
Mix requests for hard data with questions about market trends or competitive landscape.
Example: "What are the sales numbers for Drug B, and how does it compare to similar drugs in the market?"


**8. Use Conditional Queries**
Frame questions with "if-then" scenarios to explore potential outcomes.
Example: "If Drug C's price increases by 10%, how might that affect its market share?"


**9. Regular Updates**
Ask for the most recent data and inquire about how often the database is updated.
Example: "When was the last data update, and what are the latest figures for Drug D?"


**10. Feedback Loop**
If you receive unclear or incomplete information, rephrase your question or ask for clarification.
Example: "I'm not sure I understood the last response. Could you explain it in simpler terms?"


Remember, C3PO is a tool designed to assist you. The more precisely and thoughtfully you interact with it, the more valuable insights you'll gain from your analytics queries.
